Our Privacy Policy

At BuildPro.lk, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. Please read this policy carefully.

Information We Collect

We collect only basic information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Submit a custom PC build quotation request
  • Contact us via our contact form
  • Communicate with us through WhatsApp, Facebook, or phone
  • Send us an email inquiry

The information we may collect includes your name, phone number, email address, and any additional details you choose to share about your requirements or preferences.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide quotations
  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To communicate with you about your requests and orders
  • To improve our products and services
  • To send occasional updates or promotional offers (only with your consent)

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect some functionality of the website.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites such as Facebook and WhatsApp. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.
