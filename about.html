Who We Are

BuildPro.lk is a trusted computer parts and custom PC build store based in Anuradhapura, Sri Lanka. We are passionate about technology and dedicated to helping our customers build the perfect computer for their needs — whether it's for gaming, content creation, office work, education, or professional use.

Founded by a team of PC building enthusiasts, we understand that every user has unique requirements. That's why we take a personalized approach to every build, ensuring that each system is optimized for its intended purpose and budget.

What We Sell

We offer a comprehensive range of computer components and accessories from leading global brands:

  • CPUs - Intel and AMD processors for every need
  • GPUs - NVIDIA GeForce and AMD Radeon graphics cards
  • RAM - DDR4 and DDR5 memory modules
  • SSDs - NVMe and SATA solid state drives
  • Motherboards - Intel and AMD compatible boards
  • Power Supplies - Reliable PSUs for stable performance
  • Casings - Stylish and functional PC cases
  • Accessories - Keyboards, mice, monitors, and more

All products are sourced from authorized distributors and come with genuine manufacturer warranties.
